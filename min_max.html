Use the Console

In the console, invoke min_max(), use individual arguments(ex..min_max(1,2,3,4,5)) or use an array(ex..min_max([1,2,3,4,5]))

Some examples copied from console

min_max(1,23,566,99)
The Minimum of 1,23,566,99 is 1
The Maximum of 1,23,566,99 is 566

min_max([1,23,566,99])
The Minimum of 1,23,566,99 is 1
The Maximum of 1,23,566,99 is 566
